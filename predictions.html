How The Models Work

⚽ Football — Poisson Model

We fetch each team's real average goals scored and conceded from API-Football, add home advantage, then use the Poisson distribution to compute the probability of every scoreline — giving Win / Draw / Loss probabilities and the most likely results.

🏀 NBA — Elo Rating Model

Each team carries an Elo rating reflecting its strength. The rating gap (plus home-court advantage) feeds the Elo win-probability formula, converting into a projected point margin.

⚠️ Important: This is a demonstration of statistical modeling for informational and educational purposes only. This is not betting advice. Sports outcomes are inherently uncertain.
