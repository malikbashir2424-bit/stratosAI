How The Models Work

⚽ Football — Poisson Model

We estimate each team's expected goals from their attacking and defensive form, add home advantage, then use the Poisson distribution — the statistical standard for modeling goal counts — to compute the probability of every scoreline. Summing those gives Win / Draw / Loss probabilities and the most likely results.

🏀 NBA — Elo Rating Model

Each team carries an Elo rating reflecting its strength. The rating gap (plus home-court advantage) feeds the Elo win-probability formula used widely in sports analytics, and converts into a projected point margin. Stronger gap → higher win probability.

These are genuine, industry-recognized statistical methods — the same family of models used by professional analysts. The full STRATOS AI engine will extend them with live decentralized data feeds and machine-learning calibration, as described in the whitepaper.
